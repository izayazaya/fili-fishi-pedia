Dorado

Common Name: Dorado

Scientific Name: Coryphaena hippurus

Size: 1.5 m - 2 m

Weight: 10 kg - 15 kg

Season: Year-round peaking in March - August

Market Price(kg): PHP 200 - 500

Dorado is found in tropical and subtropical waters of the Philippines, particularly in offshore areas and around coral reefs. It is often found in deep waters, though it can also be spotted near floating debris or seaweed mats, where it hunts for smaller fish. Dorado is widely distributed in the Philippines, especially in regions like the Visayan Sea, Sulu Sea, and the waters surrounding Luzon.

Dorado (Coryphaena hippurus) is a commercially important fish in the Philippines, known for its vibrant color and fast growth. It is highly valued for its firm, white, and flavorful meat, making it a favorite for grilling, frying, and sashimi.
